Instructions

What to do ?

You have to mark the speech moments with bounding boxes.

How to do it ?

  • Use the left and right arrows to move the cursor, if you can press [w] to speed up.
  • The active box is in green, you can change its borders by holding [control] (start of the region) or [shift] (end of the region) and then use the arrows.
  • You can switch to another box by pressing [arrow up].
  • You can remove the active box by pressing [backspace].
  • You can create a new box by pressing [n].
  • You can play/pause the audio with the space bar.
  • You can undo your actions by pressing [u].
  • You can ignore the current sample by pressing [i].
  • When you have fnished, validate the annotation with [enter].
